Welcome !

             In this website I will show you how parenting to the fullest is done. My page also contains some of my story, challenges and tips for soon to be fathers out there ( HAHA ) . A lot of people criticize you for being a father at an early age. They can only see the wrong things that you have done. But for me, it is just one of God's challenges in life . Even though that happened to me I still did my best to be a responsible and loving dad.
             Being a teen dad requires open mindedness and sense of responsibility. At first you might not be ready yet but as time goes by you will realize that showing love and giving the needs of your child is one of the best things that you have done in your whole life. Some people will criticize you but more people will appreciate you for being a responsible dad at a young age.
