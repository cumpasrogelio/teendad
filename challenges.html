Challenges

Everything in this world has a consequence and being a father also has challenges. Let me show you some of the challenges that I am having today. This examples might give you some ideas of how to manage time and money when you become a father.  Cheers !

Unemployment

Back when I didn't have a job it's kind of frustrating because you don't know where to get money to buy the needs of your daughter. 

Limited Time

I used to spend a lot of time at school and do my homework when I get home so I only have  limited time to bond with my daughter.

Lack of Money

Sometimes I don't have enough money to buy my daughters needs . But at those times I assure to prioritize the expenses of my daughters needs before mine.
