My STory

Where It Started

Back on my teenage days, I meet this girl on facebook. She's was kinda cute, charming and intelligent. We became friends and had a lot of conversations. As day past by we feel in love with each other . We celebrated anniversary for 3 times until we were so in loved that we wanted more . Back on those days we were not that matured enough and wanted to do things on our own. Then the time came, and my girlfriend got pregnant. We were so frustrated that time and we didn't know what to do that's when we asked helped from our parents. Of course at first our parents were angry but as time goes by they guided us to right way.


​The Time My Daughter Was Born

​At first I was not quite ready to become a father but with the help of my wife and parents I slowly recognize the worth of having a daughter. When you feel down  and lonely, and see your daughter smile or laugh, the pain goes away. Life becomes more happier and more meaningful when you have someone to cherish.​


​And Today

​Right now I can surely say that I am happy with the life I chose.  With the God's gift , blessings and many good things that had happen to my life, having my daughter is the best. Nothing can replace the happiness that my daughter is giving to me. As time goes by my daughter became my motivation and my strength. And soon she well become my hope.
