TIps

Cherish Time 

One thing that will amaze you is how fast the time goes by. Cherishing your time with your child is one of the best thing you should do now. Because you'll never know sooner or later they are old enough and its too late for you to bond with them. Especially when your child is a toddler time runs really fast on those days.

Show them Love

Showing your love to your child is one of the most greatest thing to do. In this way you will feel better and your children will repay you also with love and happiness. So when they least expect it and when you’re upset, instead of yelling show them love. That's how parenting to the fullest is.

Patience

Having a child requires a lot of patience. Allowing yourself to react in anger or frustration is not the best thing for your child, and you must remember that. That means you need to take a deep breath, or a walk, when you start to lose your patience. Practice patience with your child and your relationship,so that your child will benefit over the long run.

Keep your Promises

Keeping your promises to your children will make them love you more. They will understand how important a promise is. And as human being keeping your promises is being a trustworthy person. In my case whenever my daughter cry and wants to go out and we can't because I'm busy. I make a promise to do it on other days.  

Pray to God

Praying is one of the good things your child should learn. Every night before we sleep, we always pray. Teaching my daughter to pray will make her spiritually  healthy and God-fearing. In this way she well learn to do good things as she grows-up.
